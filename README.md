INFORMATION

The website is a public-facing business site for Cal's Cupcakes, a fictional local bakery who is looking to expand their reach and client base as well as advertise their new catering service. 

NAVIGATION

Links to the pages can be found at the top in the website's navbar and are turned into a hamburger menu on smaller devices.
The main page includes the story of the business and some customer reviews, the contact page includes contact information and a map of the business's locations, the catering page includes the catering form and a system to get automatic quotes, and the pastries page includes a menu.
At the bottom of each page is a "back to top" button, and a footer that has links to all of the business's social media accounts.
