Welcome to Cal's Cupcakes!

Cal's Cupcakes is a family-owned bakery that has been serving the community for just over two years. With numerous newly opened locations across Ottawa, we specialize in creating delicious cupcakes and pastries that are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. Whether it's for a special occasion or just a daily treat, we have something for everyone.

At Cal's Cupcakes, we strive for quality and accessibility. Thus, we use only the finest ingredients to create our delectable treats. So, get ready to indulge in a world of sweetness!
